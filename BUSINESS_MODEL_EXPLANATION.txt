1. WHAT IS OUR PRODUCT?
================================================================================

   Think of it like "Google Maps, but for diseases."

   Our dashboard is a software tool (website) that shows government health
   officers WHERE a disease outbreak is about to happen — 48 HOURS BEFORE
   it actually spreads.

   It does this by combining two types of data:
     • Hospital records  → How many people are getting sick, from what disease
     • Water quality data → Is the water in that area clean or contaminated?

   When the water goes bad AND more people start getting sick at the same time,
   our system raises an alarm: "WARNING: Cholera outbreak likely in Region X
   within 48 hours."

7. ROI — RETURN ON INVESTMENT (Why It's Worth the Money)
================================================================================

   Simple math that proves this system saves money:

   WITHOUT our system (Reactive Response):
   ─────────────────────────────────────────
   • Hospital overflow cost          = Rs 1.8 Crore
   • Productivity loss (people sick) = Rs 1.2 Crore
   • Emergency medicine procurement  = Rs 50 Lakh
   • Emergency water tankers         = Rs 30 Lakh
   TOTAL COST OF OUTBREAK            = Rs 3.8 Crore

   WITH our system (Preventive Action):
   ─────────────────────────────────────────
   • Dashboard subscription          = Rs 6 Lakh/year
   • Water testing & monitoring      = Rs 12 Lakh
   • Pre-positioned medicines        = Rs 15 Lakh
   • Early alert system ops          = Rs 8 Lakh
   TOTAL COST OF PREVENTION          = Rs 41 Lakh

   SAVINGS = Rs 3.8 Crore - Rs 41 Lakh = Rs 3.39 Crore saved
   ROI = 8.5x (Every Rs 1 spent on prevention saves Rs 8.50)

   In simple terms: It is 8.5 times CHEAPER to prevent an outbreak
   than to deal with it after it happens.
