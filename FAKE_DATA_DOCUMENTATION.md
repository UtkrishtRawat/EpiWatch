## 4. How Analytics Use This Data

### Anomaly Detection (Z-Score Method)
- Counts daily cases per disease
- Flags days where count is **1.5+ standard deviations above average**

### 48-Hour Forecasting (Simple Moving Average)
- Uses 7-day moving average + trend analysis
- Predicts cases for next 24h and 24-48h
- Risk levels: Low / Medium / High / Critical

### Correlation Analysis
- Compares water quality (coliform, pH, turbidity) with disease counts per zone
- Flags zones with poor water AND high cases
